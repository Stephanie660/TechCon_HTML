Our History

TechCon started in 2010 as a small meetup for passionate innovators looking to share knowledge and collaborate on emerging technologies. Over the years, it has grown into a global platform attracting thousands of attendees annually — from tech startups to industry leaders — making it one of the most respected conferences in the technology world.

Our Mission

The mission of TechCon is to inspire innovation, foster collaboration, and promote responsible technological growth. We aim to empower communities and organizations to use technology as a force for good in shaping the future.

Notable Past Speakers

Dr. Emily Chen

A leader in artificial intelligence research, Dr. Chen is renowned for her work in ethical AI. At TechCon 2018, she delivered a keynote on AI’s role in transforming healthcare systems globally.

Sophia Martinez

A cybersecurity expert, Sophia Martinez emphasized the importance of protecting digital identities in her 2021 keynote. Her insights continue to influence best practices in data protection and online safety.
